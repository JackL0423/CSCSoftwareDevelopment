# GlobalFlavors — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 2025  
**Developer:** GlobalFlavors Team

---

## 1. Introduction

GlobalFlavors (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy.  
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the GlobalFlavors mobile application (the “App”).

By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information (Provided by You)
- Email address (via Firebase Authentication)
- Display name/profile photo (if uploaded)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (model, OS version, app version)
- Usage analytics (screens visited, feature interactions)
- Crash logs & performance metrics (via Firebase Crashlytics)
- Authentication and session data

### 2.3 User-Generated Content
- Saved recipes
- Ingredient lists
- Preferences and region selections

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide and improve core features of the App  
- Authenticate users and maintain secure accounts  
- Generate personalized recipe suggestions  
- Sync data across devices via Firebase  
- Monitor performance and troubleshoot bugs  
- Enhance user experience through analytics  

We **do not** sell or rent your personal information.

---

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We may share data only with:

- Firebase Services (Authentication, Firestore, Storage, Analytics)  
- Cloud Functions & Cloud Scheduler for recipe ingestion and updates  
- External APIs solely for fetching recipe information (no personal data is sent)  

We do **not** share your personal information with advertisers or unrelated third parties.  
We may disclose information if required by law.

---

## 5. Data Storage & Security

Your information is securely stored using:

- Google Firebase (encrypted in transit and at rest)
- Access controls that restrict who can view your data  

While we use industry-standard security methods, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. You acknowledge this risk when using the App.

---

## 6. Cookies / Tracking Technologies

GlobalFlavors does **not** use browser cookies.  
However, it uses:

- Firebase Analytics event tracking  
- Device identifiers for performance metrics  

These tools help us understand usage patterns and improve the App.
